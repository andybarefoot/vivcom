Syrill Kizza: Executive director and founder

My name is Syrill Kizza, a Social Entrepreneur, Motivational speaker and a child rights advocate. Born on 17th December - 1989 in Kampala (Uganda) and raised in a slum area as an Orphan, Syrill found life extremely difficult, being the last born of 4 siblings Syrill was exposed to a lot of child hood oppression.

With such a restive past Syrill is extremely passionate about helping vulnerable families and children dwelling in slum communities to offset the enormous challenges they face with a robust belief that they can succeed and become responsible leaders tomorrow.

To Syrill charity is a calling to proclaim good news to the poor, brokenhearted and the hurting world, "Christ asked us to show mercy to the poor, the sick and the needy, this in effect is ministering to Him personally and we shall be rewarded accordingly." (Mathew 25:35-40).
