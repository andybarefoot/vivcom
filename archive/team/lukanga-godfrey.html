Lukanga Musoke Godfrey: Treasurer

My name is Godfrey Lukanga Musoke, a born of 22nd-June-1990 in Kampala slums, being born and raised in a slum community Godfrey was confronted with lots of child hood challenges like child labor and oppression brought about by the intricate behaviors characteristic of such communities.

Godfrey is extremely enthusiastic about offsetting abject poverty in slum areas which has critically championed people's choices in life, by rendering them as an inferior component society, he is therefore passionate to serve such marginalized families by impacting them for defensible growth which can secure families and the children therein.
