Abraham Olupot: Programs Director

My name is Abraham Olupot a born of 1st-January-1989 and raised in Kampala Slums. Charity to me is honoring God's calling upon my life and a way to walk and follow the footsteps of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, who commends us to love God and also our neighbors with all the heart, spirit and soul.

It's true you cannot love without giving, I have therefore devoted to giving my time, influence, energy and resources to helping the less privileged and critically vulnerable members in the slum communities in my society. Having grown up and bred in a slum community Abraham is extremely zealous to see a transformed society where families better their Standards of living, Children can obtain basic necessities and families are empowered to be self-reliant.

"When you beat your olive trees and gather the grapes of your vineyard, you shall not strip it afterward. It shall be for the sojourner, the fatherless, and the widow."" (Deuteronomy 24:17-21)
